Ticket Tags

TeamSupport provides the ability to add one ore more tag keywords to any ticket. The keywords that have been added to tickets are displayed in the Ticket Tag window on the right side, and clicking on one will show all tickets that have that tag associated with them.



Tags are also used in our "Ticket Deflection" technology which prompts your customers with knowledge base articles when they submit a ticket.



In addition tag keywords are highlighted in ticket actions and you can click on those keywords to see related issues. This will help your agents find related tickets that may be beneficial in resolving your customer issues.
