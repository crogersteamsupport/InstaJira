Welcome to TeamSupport.com!  TeamSupport is a diverse, flexible, ticket management application that supports a wide spectrum of capabilities to handle everything from basic issue management, to an entire customer service enterprise solution. TeamSupport can manage the day to day issues as well as bugs, features and tasks and their relationship to your customers and products.

TeamSupport is uniquely suited to work within multiple departments within your organization:  Customer service, product management, quality assurance, and product development.
