Knowledge Base

This section lists all tickets that have been added to the Knowledge Base. Any ticket can be assigned to the Knowledge Base which lets your internal users have a place to reference common issues. If the ticket is marked as "visible to customers" then your customers can view the tickets either via the Public Knowledge base or the Advanced Portal.

The Knowledge base is also a great place to segregate and display helpful information such as how to configure products or various protocols that should be followed.
