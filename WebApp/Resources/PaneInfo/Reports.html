Reports

TeamSupport offers advanced reporting capabilities which you can use to view your data in virtually any way you want.



Several pre-defined reports come standard as a part of your TeamSupport subscription, and you can also create custom repots with ease. You have access to every field, including any custom fields you add for reporting.



It's also important to note that you can put any report here on your dashboard so that critical data is always available to you.
