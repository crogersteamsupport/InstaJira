Groups

Groups are a very powerful function in TeamSupport. When a user is a member of a group, and a ticket is assigned to that group but not assigned to an individual, all members of that group get notified.

You can create as many groups as you like. A user can be a member of multiple groups as well. Typical groups may be support, level 2 support, product development, quality assurance, etc. Once the groups are created, simply assign the correct people to these groups. When a ticket is created, but the creator does not know the specific individual to assign the ticket to, they can simply assign it to the correct group - All members of that group will be notified, and any of them can take ownership on the ticket.
