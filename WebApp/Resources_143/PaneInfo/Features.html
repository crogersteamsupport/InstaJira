Features

This grid displays all of the Feature tickets. A feature is a distinguishing characteristic of a product (e.g., performance, portability, or functionality). Another unique difference between TeamSupport and many other solutions is the ability to intelligently track feature requests made by your customers.

This is very useful for understanding the needs of your customers, and giving product managers and developers a location to store this information for road mapping purposes.
