Ticket Detail

The Ticket Detail page shows a great deal of information about the issue and is where you will modify the ticket and add actions to it.

Logging Actions

To add a new comment, or “action” as we refer to it, to the ticket, simple click the “Log a New Action” button. This will display a text box where you can you add more information about what it being done to solve the issue. You can also add attachments here as well.

If you check the “Visible to Customers” checkbox when adding a new action, any customers who are associated with the ticket will automatically get e-mailed your comment (note that you can modify the e-mail that goes out via E-mail Templates – Click here for more information).

Lots of Detail!

On the right side of the Ticket Detail window you can track a lot of information. You can assign the ticket to an individual or group, change the status, associate customers, add tags, relate the ticket to other tickets, and much more. You can also add your own custom data fields to the ticket (see here for more detail on custom fields).

There’s a lot of information available in the ticket detail page, and to learn more about it please read our documentation located here.
