Dashboard

The dashboard allows you to place your most commonly used information in one location. It is fully customizable to allow you to present whatever information is most appropriate to your operations.

To open a ticket simply click on the blue folder icon and the ticket will open in a tab.

To change the layout of the dashboard use your mouse to drag and drop the windows and place them in the order that you want. You can also get rid of windows that are not useful to you by clicking on the X to remove them.

You can add any report from the “Reports” section of TeamSupport by clicking on the “Add Report” button in the upper right – This will present a list of reports and let you select where you want it placed on the dashboard.
