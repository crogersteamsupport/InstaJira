My Tickets

View all of the tickets that are assigned to you. You can see your open tickets, closed tickets and all tickets regardless of status.

You can preview any ticket by selecting one in the grid. If you would like to make changes to a ticket, simply click the blue folder icon in the grid.

Columns may be sorted by simply clicking on the column title.
